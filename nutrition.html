Nutrition

Simple meal plans and recipes to fuel your fitness journey.

Tip of the Day

"Don't skip breakfast. A protein-rich morning meal helps stabilize energy levels and reduces cravings throughout the day."

Meal Plans

High Protein

  • Breakfast: Eggs and oatmeal
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken and rice
  • Snack: Greek yogurt
  • Dinner: Salmon and veggies

Balanced

  • Breakfast: Avocado toast with eggs
  • Lunch: Turkey wrap with salad
  • Snack: Apple with peanut butter
  • Dinner: Pasta with lean meat sauce

Low Calorie

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with protein powder
  • Lunch: Salad with grilled chicken
  • Snack: Veggies and hummus
  • Dinner: Zucchini noodles with turkey

Protein Pancakes
